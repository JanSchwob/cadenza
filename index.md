# Privacy Policy — Cadenza

Last updated: May 2026

Cadenza ("the App") is a cycling tracking application for iOS.

## Data We Collect
- **Location data**: Used for GPS tracking during rides and route planning. 
  Not stored on external servers.
- **Health & fitness data**: Heart rate and activity data processed locally 
  on your device only.
- **Music**: Spotify integration uses OAuth authentication. 
  No credentials are stored by the App.

## Data Storage
All ride data is stored locally on your device. 
Cadenza does not operate its own servers and does not transmit 
personal data to third parties.

## Third-Party Services
- **Spotify**: Subject to Spotify's own Privacy Policy 
  (spotify.com/privacy)
- **Apple HealthKit**: Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy 
  (apple.com/privacy)
- **Mapbox / routing provider**: Subject to the respective 
  provider's privacy policy

## Your Rights
You can delete all app data at any time by removing the App 
from your device.
